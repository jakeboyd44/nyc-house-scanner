# Jake's House Music Taste Profile

This is an example taste profile. Fork it and make it yours.

## Must-See Artists

These artists (or similar-sounding acts) are Tier 1 matches:

**UK House / Tech House:**
Ben Sterling, Prospa, LF System, Eli Brown, Patrick Topping, Archie Hamilton,
Carlita, Josh Baker, Rossi., Butch, Laurence Guy, Kettama, Ewan McVicar,
Honey Dijon, Jamie Jones, Franky Rizardo, Solardo, SOSA, Ben Hemsley

**Bigger Acts (still interested):**
Fisher, John Summit, Chris Lake, Dom Dolla

**Eclectic / Deep / Disco-Adjacent:**
Palms Trax, Avalon Emerson, Motor City Drum Ensemble, DJ Harvey

**Wildcard — always alert:**
Any UK house/tech house DJ doing a rare NYC popup or one-off

## Promoters / Collectives

- **Raw Cuts** (@rawcuts.tv) — TOP PRIORITY. Any event = Tier 1 alert. Check Shotgun first.
- **Golden Record NYC** — warehouse events in East Williamsburg
- **Nowadays** — Ridgewood, especially "Nonstop" all-night events
- **Project 91** — NYC house events
- **MAD Radio NYC** — Shotgun-based house/disco nights
- **Teksupport** — major NYC promoter, warehouse-scale events (Ben Sterling b2b Hot Since 82, Loco Dice b2b Seth Troxler). Posts on RA, Shotgun, and DICE. Tier 1 when lineup matches.
- **Saborparty** — check for house/tech house lineups

## Genre Keywords

**Match (include):**
house, tech house, UK house, deep house, minimal house, acid house, garage, bassline

**Reject (exclude):**
dubstep, drum & bass, hardstyle, trance, big room EDM, psytrance, riddim, trap

## Vibe Indicators

The "Raw Cuts NYC" vibe is the gold standard:
- Intimate warehouse/popup events
- No-phones policy
- Community-driven
- Limited capacity
- RSVP/Shotgun ticketing
- Location revealed day-of

Events matching 3+ of these vibe indicators get bumped up one tier.
